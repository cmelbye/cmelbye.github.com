Tips & Tricks

Learn how to effectively use GPAide with these tips. If you have any unanswered questions, feel free to send an email.

Recalculate your GPA

To recalculate your GPA by changing a grade in one of your classes, simply tap the class on the Courses page.

This makes it easy and fast to experiment and see which grade you need in a class to attain a certain GPA.

Use your school's grade scale

To accommodate different grade scales at different schools, it's possible to select not only normal letter grades, but also A+, A-, B+, B-, etc.

To ensure that your calculated GPA is accurate, choose only grades that your school officially uses.
